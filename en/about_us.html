About us

This site was created as part of a pedagogical project by Angelo Simoes, a first-year student in the MMI (Multimedia and Internet) department of the IUT of Belfort-Montbéliard. This project is called "Notes for later", the basic premise was to present to our future selves different elements which are :

  • A place
  • A book
  • A film
  • A music
  • A regret of our generation
  • A hope of our generation

My way of approaching this exercise is to talk about things that particularly pleased me when I was young and that I can go back to in the future for nostalgia.
